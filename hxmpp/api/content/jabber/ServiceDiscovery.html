class jabber.ServiceDiscovery

Manages discovery of services from XMPP entities. Two kinds of information can be discovered: The identity and capabilities of an entity, including the protocols and features it supports. The items associated with an entity, such as the list of rooms hosted at a multi-user chat service.

XEP 30 - ServiceDiscovery: http://www.xmpp.org/extensions/xep-0030.html
