class jabber.lop.Farm

Available on all platforms

Manages the spawning of virtual machines. The disco 'identity' of a farm MUST be of category="client" and type="bot" (name is up to the implementation).

Linked Process Protocol: http://xmpp.org/extensions/inbox/lop.html
